1.
Product Vision
**Current idea:** Notes app with voice input + LLM structuring.
**Elevated vision:** **SmartNote AI is your second brain that actively learns how you think.** It captures fleeting thoughts in any form—voice rambles, messy text, photos of whiteboards—and transforms them into interconnected knowledge that grows smarter over time. Unlike passive note apps, SmartNote AI understands context, surfaces relevant past notes when you need them, and helps you see connections you'd never notice alone.
**One-liner:** "Capture chaos. Retrieve clarity."

2.
Innovative Features (Beyond "AI for AI's sake")
| Feature | Why It's Genuinely Useful |
|---------|--------------------------|
| **Thought Threading** | Links related notes automatically across days/weeks—surfaces "You wrote something similar on Oct 3" |
| **Learning Mode** | Generates quiz questions from your notes to reinforce retention (spaced repetition) |

3.
Problem Statement
**The core problem:** Our best ideas come at inconvenient moments—in the shower, during commutes, half-asleep. By the time we can properly write them down, we've lost 80% of the insight. Traditional note apps require you to organize *while* capturing, which kills spontaneity.
**Real-world examples:**
A student attends a lecture, takes messy notes, then spends 2 hours reformatting them—or never reviews them at all
A founder has a breakthrough idea at 2 AM, types gibberish into their phone, can't understand it the next day
A professional takes meeting notes but can never find them when relevant topics come up months later

8.
Risks & Mitigations
| Risk | Mitigation |
|------|------------|
| LLM latency kills flow | Show immediate transcription, structure in background |
| Overstructuring loses voice | Always preserve original, show structured as "suggestion" |
| Privacy concerns | Local-first option, clear data policies, on-device processing for sensitive notes |
| Feature creep | Ruthless MVP focus—nail voice→structure before adding bells |
| Cost of API calls | Cache aggressively, batch operations, offer limited free tier |
